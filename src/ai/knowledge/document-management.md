# Ogeemo Document Management System

## The Dual-Mirror System
Ogeemo acts as a Structural Layer to end file chaos in cloud storage. The Ogeemo Method involves maintaining a structural mirror in Ogeemo that points directly to your storage in Google Drive. This ensures every client file, contract, and image is always precisely where it belongs.

## Setup & The 13 Protected Folders
To establish the bridge:
1. Create a master folder named **"Ogeemo"** in your Google Drive.
2. Inside that folder, you must mirror the 13 "Protected" folders that are hardcoded into Ogeemo:
   - Clients
   - Family
   - Friends
   - Miscellaneous
   - Ogeemo Users
   - Prospects
   - Suppliers
   - Contract Workers
   - Employee Workers
   - Images
   - Marketing
   - Ogeemo Notes
   - Knowledge Base
3. Link them by copying the share link from Google Drive and pasting it into the "Link Google Drive Folder" option in the Ogeemo Document Manager.

## High-Fidelity Naming Protocol
To ensure files are chronologically sorted and easily searchable across the Dual-Mirror System, always use the following naming convention when saving files and PDFs (including emails):

**Format**: `YYYYMMDD Client Name, Subject, Initial, v#`
**Example**: `20260225 John Smith Subject line JS and v1`

This protocol prevents "Assumptive Liability" and guarantees data is organized and easily accessible.
