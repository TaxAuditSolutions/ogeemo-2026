# Ogeemo Intelligence Core: The Master Mind

## Overview
Ogeemo is a high-fidelity operational engine and "Spider Web" of business intelligence. It is designed to act as a unified terminal for managing Finances, Relationships, Projects, and AI Ingestion.

## Core Philosophical Modes
- **Orchestration**: The process of managing multiple business threads (Ledgers, Tasks, Contacts) from a single command point.
- **The Golden Gate**: The secure, crash-proof entry point that handles user redirection based on identity.
- **The Master Mind**: The unified dashboard where all operational signals converge.

## Interface Elements: Action Chips
Action Chips are atomic operational units used across the platform to trigger specific workflows:
- **Ledger Chips**: Access Income and Expense ledgers.
- **Task Chips**: Interact with the Command Centre timeline.
- **AI Chips**: Trigger Dispatch intelligence for deep analysis.

## Operational Modules
1. **Financial Hub**: Manages Gen-2 Ledgers, A/R (Invoicing), A/P (Bills), and Payroll run orchestration. 
2. **Command Centre**: A visual, drag-and-drop orchestration board for Tasks, Events, and Reminders. 
3. **AI Dispatch**: The primary natural language interface for executing platform commands and querying the knowledge base.

## Status Levels
- **Apprentice**: New members of the Ogeemo Circle who are mastering the BKS protocols.
- **Mentor**: Certified advisors who have mastered the "Black Box of Evidence."
