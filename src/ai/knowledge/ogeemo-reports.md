# Ogeemo Reports Hub

## Overview
The Ogeemo Reports Hub is where raw operational tracking data—originating from the Command Centre, Gen-2 Ledger, and Action Chips—is compiled into coherent summaries. By combining the "Spider Web" of business intelligence into exportable formats, Ogeemo ensures you remain completely Audit Ready.

## Core Reports

### 1. Client Statement
**What is the Client Statement?**
The Client Statement is a comprehensive, transparent report generated for a specific client that aggregates professional timing, billable actions, and general ledger activity. 
When tasks, events, or projects are initiated and toggled as "Billable" from the Command Centre calendar, that professional timing is automatically captured and synced to the Client Statement. It serves as an exact record of the time and resources devoted to their file, ensuring there is no "Assumptive Liability."

### 2. Work Activity Summary
A high-level view of all operational actions. It translates the granular 5-minute slots of the Command Centre and the status of projects in the Action Manager into a digestible summary of overall business output and progression.

### 3. Worker Time Log Report
A detailed chronological ledger of the exact time tracked by internal contract workers and employees. This prevents "invisible work" from falling through the cracks and acts as the foundation for clean Payroll orchestration.

### 4. Client Time Log Report
A segmented analysis of time expenditure categorized strictly by the client. It helps identify resource drains, ensuring that time dedicated to specific clients—whether for sales leads or finalized contracts—is profitable and accurately logged.
