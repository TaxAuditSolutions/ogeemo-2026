# Quick Navigation

## What is Quick Navigation?
Quick Navigation (also known as the customizable action chip menu) is a personalized menu system found in areas like the Accounting Hub and HR Hub. It allows users to create their own custom dashboard of shortcuts. Users can curate their most-used links and actions, avoiding the need to search through standard menus.

## How to Manage Quick Navigation
Users can manage their Quick Navigation by clicking the "Manage Navigation" button (e.g., at `/accounting/manage-navigation` or `/hr-manager/manage-navigation`).

The management interface features a drag-and-drop system with the following capabilities:
1. **Adding Items**: Drag items from the "Available Items" panel up into the "Selected Items" panel.
2. **Reordering Items**: Click and drag chips within the "Selected Items" panel to establish the desired sequence.
3. **Removing Items**: Drag an item from "Selected Items" back down to the "Available Items" panel.
4. **Deleting Custom Items**: Drag an item to the "Trash" zone or use the 3-dot menu to select "Delete" to permanently remove a custom link.
5. **Adding Custom Links**: Click "+ Add Custom Link" to define a new shortcut capable of linking to internal Ogeemo pages or external websites.

**Important Note:** Changes made to the Selected Items layout are not saved automatically. Users must click the **"Save Order"** button to finalize and apply their new custom Quick Navigation layout.
